Its 2007.

You are driving down an empty road with nothing as far as the eye can see on each side. It is peaceful. You are listening to music --- your favorite CD -- with the windows down. The smell of the earth, the sun-baked grass, and the hot summer dirt waft into your car. The sun is hot, but the breeze blasting in through the open windows cools you off before you can realize. You feel at peace...
